Freak Fortress 2

5/18/2015

0 Comments

 
This will be the longest post, but it will also be the only one on the subject. I made some Freak Fortress 2 bosses recently, with all custom abilities. I'll leave this link here for downloads, since this is where everything will be put that I want public.
Now for the bosses!

The Rock: Not what you are thinking, this boss started out from me watching a video that happened to have a really cool concept, this video in fact.
He functions almost identically, when he "rages", he becomes knockback immune and can walk through players, gibbing them in the process. His melee crits against non burning enemies, which he can't ignite, so he will always crit, and kiling with a crit will gib players. The punch does 176 damage, or just enough to kill a non-overhealed Demo.

RubberFruit: This is a boss I just had to do, he was so simple to create, his rage does just what the freak does, steal things. It has a chance to steal primary or secondary weapons, and also has a chance to steal sentries! As of writing this, he has a missing feature which I don't intend to make just yet, which is him being able to use stolen weapons, and stealing Demoknight's shields.

Solgineer: Another more simpler one, he get's a charged ability in place of his superjump that simple fires a crocket in the direction he's looking, it costs 10 rage to do so, and is on a 10 second cooldown(Which makes the rage cost almost redundant). For a rage, he get's to instantly teleport onto someone and get's invulnerability for 3 seconds.

All the other abilities I've made are too vast to fully explain, so I'll leave that for you to read on the AlliedModders thread, since I keep that up to date and detailed.
