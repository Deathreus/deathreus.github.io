I make sourcemod plugins and code in C++
﻿

I'm a self taught independent developer, who codes primarily in SourcePawn and C++ and makes plugins for servers for the game Team Fortress 2. I do take requests, check my steam out which you can find a link to in the Contact page, I accept any form of payment, or none at all depending on how easy it is. It should be known that I don't have a limitless knowledge of this, I'm completely self taught and lack the ability to do anything hugely complex.

I do host a couple servers, you can find more info about this in the Servers page, but for a short summary, I have a Freak Fortress 2 server with bosses made by me, a Mann vs Machine server that holds 10 players, and a TF2x10 server with some custom additions. Until I have a real source of income, these are home hosted servers, and won't be 100% stable as far as lag and uptime.

As for myself personally, I'm a born smartass, I'm shy, I'm anti-social, I'm basically your average basement dwelling nerd only without the basement. I'm a kind person, almost too kind, and when I know someone well enough, I won't stop talking. I don't typically like to talk, but I can't stop when I know them.
